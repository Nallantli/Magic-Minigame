Instructions

Every level contains an exit room that is locked - defeat all enemies on a given level to unlock the door and advance.

Each level has health coins and new spells that can be picked up in random rooms. Before you advance you should pick up each spell.

Spells

You control a wizard who conveys one of the four classical elements: air, fire, water, earth.

To access all known spells, click the escape key. Here you can add up to four of each to your deck.

In a fight the deck is randomly sorted and distributed at the bottom. Select the spell, and the target to use.

Enemies (and you) will have resistance to the same element, and vulnerability to the opposing element.

Fire opposes Water, Air opposes Earth
